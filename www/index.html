This is your sign to learn Filipino Sign Language in an engaging and fun way!

CHOOSE YOUR DIFFICULTY

Choose Your Level of Difficulty

Easy: Guess the phrase by flipping 2 cards

Intermediate: Guess the phrase by flipping 3 cards

Hard: Guess the phrase by flipping 4 cards

Memorize the Phrase

Each card contains a word. Your goal is to flip the cards in the correct sequence to match the given phrase.

Flip the Cards

Tap on any two cards to reveal their words. If the cards match the correct sequence, you proceed to the next round. If not, all cards will be facedown again, and you'll have to guess the sequence once more.

Race Against the Time

You have 60 seconds to complete all the rounds within your chosen difficulty level. Keep an eye on the timer!

Earn Stars

After each completed round, you will earn stars based on the difficulty level:

Easy: 1 star

Intermediate: 2 stars

Hard: 3 stars

Powerup with Stars

You can use the stars you've earned to buy power-ups that can assist you during the game:

Hint (Cost: 2 stars): Reveals one correct card randomly.

Reveal (Cost: 3 stars): Shows all correct cards, but not their sequence.

Time Freeze (Cost: 2 stars): Pauses the timer for 3 seconds.

Aim for a High Score

Your score is determined by the time remaining at the end of each difficulty level. The faster you complete the rounds, the higher your score!

Send Your Score to the Leaderboards

After finishing a difficulty level, you can check your score and submit it to the leaderboards. See how you stack up against other players!
